Michael Jordan

The greatest basketball player of all time.

Up above is an image of Michael Jordan playing basketball on his most iconic team, the chicago bulls.

Here's a time line of Jordan's life:

  • 1963 - Born on February 17 in Brooklyn, New York.
  • 1982 – Hits game-winning shot to win NCAA Championship with UNC.
  • 1984 – Drafted by the Chicago Bulls; wins Olympic gold with Team USA. Michael Jordan signs with Nike, launching the Air Jordan sneaker line.
  • 1985 – First Air Jordan sneaker, the Air Jordan 1, is released, becoming an instant cultural icon.
  • 1991 to 1993 – Wins three consecutive NBA Championships with the Bulls.
  • 1993 – Retires after his father’s death and briefly plays baseball.
  • 1995 – Returns to the NBA with “I’m back.”
  • 1996 to 1998 – Wins another three-peat (1996, 1997, 1998); hits iconic game-winner in 1998 Finals.
  • 2001 to 2003 – Plays two final seasons with the Washington Wizards.
  • 2009 – Inducted into the Basketball Hall of Fame.
  • 2010 – Becomes majority owner of the Charlotte Hornets, the NBA’s first Black majority owner.
  • 2020 – Speaks at Kobe Bryant’s memorial, showing his impact on generations.
  • Today – Regarded as the greatest basketball player of all time and a global icon of excellence and competitiveness.
